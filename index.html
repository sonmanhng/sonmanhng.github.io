Quantum Minds, Rebel Science

I'm a senior student at the University of Science, Vietnam National University, serving as research lead at the AI – Analytical Chemistry Laboratory under Assoc. Prof. Dr. Ta Thi Thao and a member of the Natural Product Chemistry Laboratory under Prof. Dr. Phan Minh Giang.

My work sits at the intersection of quantum computing, machine learning, and analytical chemistry, building computational tools that accelerate scientific discovery. I believe the most exciting science happens at the boundary between disciplines.
